Audit Objective

The audit is intended to assess the organization's current level of cybersecurity readiness in accordance with the applicable regulatory requirements, identify gaps and weaknesses, and provide a structured basis for further remediation, risk reduction, and preparation for external assurance.

Evaluation Method

Responses are reviewed against the applicable regulatory requirements and control objectives. The final assessment reflects the reviewed state after the administrator validates the submitted answers, notes, and supporting evidence.

The evaluation considers the completeness, effectiveness, and quality of implemented controls and documented evidence.

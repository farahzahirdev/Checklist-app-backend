2.2   Audit Objective

The objective of the audit is to assess the current level of cybersecurity preparedness of the organization in accordance with applicable regulatory requirements, identify gaps and weaknesses, and provide a structured basis for further remediation, risk reduction, and preparation for external assurance.

2.3   Assessment Method

Responses are evaluated in accordance with applicable regulatory requirements and control objectives. The resulting assessment reflects the reviewed status after verification of submitted answers, notes, and supporting evidence by the administrator. The assessment takes into account the completeness, effectiveness, and quality of implemented measures and documented evidence.

2.4   Response Scale

Fully implemented
(YES)		 The requirement is fully implemented, consistently applied in practice, and supported by appropriate and sufficient evidence.
Partially implemented
(PARTIALLY)		 The requirement is implemented to a limited extent. There are gaps in implementation, consistency, or supporting evidence.
Mostly Not Implemented
(MOSTLY NO)		 The requirement is mostly not implemented. There are only isolated measures or implementation is inadequate and unsystematic.
Not implemented
(NO)		 The requirement is not implemented at all. No relevant measures or evidence are available.
